Seaside Strike is my third game from the Udemy course I've been taking over the Summer. It's a simple demo of a tower defense game, but what I found most fun about this game was making my own assets. I used the program MagicaVoxel, recommended by the teacher of the course, and I had a great time making my own beach related objects. My favorite part of this game is the voxel art style, as I think it looks very neat and creates a cozy world for the player to enjoy. Click here for a playable version of the game!
